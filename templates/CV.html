Automated Detection of Patient Mobility

Problem Statement

The problem at hand involves detecting patient mobility issues using video monitoring. Many patients, especially the elderly or those recovering from surgery, face mobility challenges that may not be easily noticed. Traditional observation methods are not always reliable, leading to delayed interventions.

Who

Patients (especially elderly and post-surgery), healthcare providers, hospital administrators, caregivers, and AI system developers.

What

The problem is the difficulty in reliably detecting patient mobility issues using traditional methods. The solution is an AI-powered video monitoring system that detects mobility problems in real-time.

Where

Hospital wards, nursing homes, long-term care facilities, and patient homes for remote monitoring.

Why

Early detection is crucial for timely intervention, improving patient safety, reducing the burden on healthcare staff, and enabling personalized care.
