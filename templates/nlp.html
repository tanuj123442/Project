AI Chatbot for Real-Time Symptom Tracking and Support

Problem Statement

The problem involves creating an AI chatbot for real-time symptom tracking and support. Patients need immediate assistance when experiencing symptoms, and traditional healthcare systems may not provide timely responses.

Who

Patients, healthcare providers, hospital administrators, AI developers, and caregivers.

What

The problem is the lack of timely symptom support for patients. The solution is an AI chatbot that provides real-time symptom tracking and guidance, escalating to healthcare professionals as needed.

Where

Mobile apps, telemedicine platforms, hospital systems, and home environments for patient care.

Why

To provide immediate support for patients, enhance self-management, monitor patients remotely, and reduce healthcare system workload by managing non-critical symptoms through AI.
